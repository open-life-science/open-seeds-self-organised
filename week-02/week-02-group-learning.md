[HOST] (10 min) [‚è∞ 70]

**Breakout activity**: Instructions for the room:

* Last week you started to draft your vision statement, in your breakout room articulate your big idea, vision or dream that you want to achieve by working openly, and experiment with expressing your vision in a short format.
* Discuss if any improvements can be made to make vision statement as clear as possible.
